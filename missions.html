Mission Timeline

Chandrayaan-2 (2019)

India's ambitious lunar exploration mission, aiming to explore the south pole of the Moon.

Mars Orbiter Mission (Mangalyaan) (2013)

First Asian spacecraft to orbit Mars! Demonstrating ISRO's ability to conduct interplanetary missions.

Imaginary Galactic Probe (2026)

A futuristic probe (creative example) to study exoplanets and black holes!

ISRO Space Station (Dream Project)

A conceptual Indian space station for future generations to research long-term space dwelling.
